## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why won't you support [Insert Speech Tool Here]?  
  Rather than try to be inclusive or worry about leaving some tool out of the equation, the plugin only offers the 
  "direct" Indigo speech hook; allowing for some speech facility to be offered. The plugin is designed to support the 
  construction of complex announcement strings rather than their actual speech.
